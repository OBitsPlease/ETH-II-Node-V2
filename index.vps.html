// THE STORY

WHY ETH II

Original Ethereum built its empire on the backs of miners. GPU rigs humming in garages and warehouses around the world — these were the machines that validated every transaction, secured every block, and turned an idea into a global financial system.

The Merge didn't just change a consensus mechanism. It turned its back on the builders who created that empire. The miners who had invested in hardware, electricity, and unwavering belief were discarded overnight — the very people who built crypto's most legendary era, cast aside.

Our mission is to reunite those builders and construct an even larger empire on top of the fallen one. To let miners and developers take what was always rightfully theirs. To reignite the machines that once defined an entire industry and give back the power of the blockchain to the people who earned it.

Miners — unite. Reclaim the decentralized spirit and the raw computational power that built the foundation of crypto's most legendary era. The machines are back. The empire rises again.

II

THE NAME

The Roman numeral II is an easter egg and a play on history. The Roman Republic turned its back on the people, and those people rose up and built the Roman Empire on top of the fallen Republic. ETH II is built on the foundation of ETH — much like the Romans did.
